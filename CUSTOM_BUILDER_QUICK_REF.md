## Pro Tips 💡

✅ **DO:**
- Be specific with motorcycle model
- Experiment with different combinations
- Try various colors before deciding
- Download multiple designs to compare

❌ **DON'T:**
- Leave motorcycle field empty
- Use generic terms like "sport bike"
- Rush the selection process
- Forget to download your favorites

## Troubleshooting 🔧

**Problem:** Button won't generate
**Solution:** Enter motorcycle brand

**Problem:** Color looks wrong
**Solution:** Use hex code for precise colors

**Problem:** Takes too long
**Solution:** First time is slower (20-30s), wait patiently

**Problem:** Result doesn't match
**Solution:** Try different finish or additional design
